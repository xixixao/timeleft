Time Left

Go back.

Configuration

You can configure the page using URL params:

  • sunrise – the start time of your day, in the format h:m, such as 6:40
  • sunset – the ending time of your day, in the format h:m, such as 19:15
  • startAge – the starting age from which you want to count, a number such as 10
  • birthDay – your birth day in the format y-m-d, such as 1989-7-17
  • endAge – the life expectancy age, a number such as 80

Examples

Born in 1983, with life expectancy of 82 years, wakes up at 9am and goes to bed at midnight:
?birthday=1983-07-04&sunrise=9:00&sunset=23:59&endAge=82&startAge=6
